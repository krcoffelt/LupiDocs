ABOUT US

Christine Leninger launched Lupi Docs & Designs to guide authors from draft to self-publish with clarity, integrity, and momentum. She partners with trusted, fellow book publishing and design experts and marketers so every book is remarkable; your unique voice amplified for your desired audience. We specialize in memoirs, novels, and books to promote your business expertise. Your book rights, and your royalties. Always.

The Lupi name honors her Jack Russell Terrier-eager, strategic, loyal, and energetic. We limit intake to 2-5 book programs per month to keep every project personal.
